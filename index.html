Family Law

How do I file for divorce in California?

To file for divorce in California, you need to submit a Petition for Dissolution of Marriage to your county's Superior Court. California is a no-fault divorce state, meaning you don't need to prove wrongdoing. There's a 6-month residency requirement before filing.

Personal Injury

What should I do after a car accident that wasn't my fault?

After a car accident that wasn't your fault, ensure everyone's safety, call the police, exchange information with the other driver, document the scene with photos, seek medical attention even for minor injuries, notify your insurance, and consider consulting a personal injury attorney.

Immigration

How can I apply for a green card through marriage?

To apply for a green card through marriage to a U.S. citizen, file Form I-130 (Petition for Alien Relative) and Form I-485 (Adjustment of Status) concurrently if you're in the U.S. legally. You'll need to provide evidence of a bona fide marriage and attend an interview with USCIS.
